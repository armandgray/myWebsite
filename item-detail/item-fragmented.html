Fragmented

An Android Developer Podcast

An Android developer podcast created by two amazing Android engineers: Donn Felker & Kaushik Gopal. The hosts have published roughly 70 episodes all talking about "about building good software and becoming better Android developers." Fragmented stands out as the top podcast for Android developers and is easily the best listen for a morning or afternoon commute. Episodes start with an introduction to the podcast and testing on Android and cover everything from RxJava to 2016 Google IO interviews. Fragmented is a bi-weekly show discussing "platform, tools, patterns and practices that help a developer go from good to amazing." Fragmented is a huge bonus to all Android Developers out there whether you are working through your first app or looking back at years of experience.
