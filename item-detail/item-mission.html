Mission Techies

Learn, Connect, Inspire...

Mission Techies is a 12-week training program (winter, spring, fall) or for summers, a 6-week intensive program on the tech field. Training is 240 hours every Monday to Friday from 1pm-5pm. The program is designed and effective in providing pathways for its diverse members of color to access opportunities in the tech industry. In its 5th year since its inception, program director Leonardo Sosa has gathered the support of Google, Uber, and Pinterest community members. Mission Techies is growing consistently with an increasingly loyal following as the program continues to prove highly fruitful in developing key learning skills for its participants and providing lasting connections to members of the tech community.
